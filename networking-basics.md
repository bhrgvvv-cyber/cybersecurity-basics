## TCP vs UDP

**TCP**
- Reliable
- Connection-based
- Slower

**UDP**
- Faster
- No connection
- Less reliable

## Common Ports

- 80 → HTTP
- 443 → HTTPS
- 21 → FTP
- 22 → SSH
